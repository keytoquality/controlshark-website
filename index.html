Prevent tomorrow's problems today

ControlShark is a cloud based statistical process control software that allows you to easily collect and analyse data from anywhere in your organisation. You can visualize your data in realtime with automated control and capability charts that are powered by AI analytics.

Whether you are providing a product or service to consumers or other businesses, any sized organisation from any industry can achieve quality success and customer satisfaction with ControlShark.

Improve your quality and performance with ControlShark

ControlShark improves your organisation's quality and performance by carrying out Process Control and Process Capability analysis. It's the perfect partner to your existing quality assurance and control programs, whatever your size of organisation or industry.

Collect Data

Simple and user friendly data collection you can instantly deploy in any work environment. ControlShark stores your tolerances so you don't waste time re-entering information. Additional benefits also include a "go/no go" gauge feature.

Analyze Data

Collected data can be visualized in realtime through your organisation dashboard where you can see the performance of all your processes in one place. Process control and capability charts along with performance metrics are automatically generated for your data.

Take Action based on Data

The results are analysed using our AI algorithms which provide key insights into problem patterns and trends to help you start achieving your quality goals right away. You will be able to interpret the results and take valuable action, even if you don't have a background in statistics.

ControlShark features

Organisation Dashboard

The organisation dashboard shows the realtime performance of all your processes in one convenient place.

Control Charts

ControlShark automatically selects the correct control chart for your data. Smart interactive charts include Intuitive zoom control for visualizing large amounts of data and hover over for more details.

Capability Plots

See improvement in real-time by visualizing the data distribution 

Go / No Go Gauge Data Entry

Deploy simple operator facing data collection with value adding go / no go gauge functionality.

Phase Functionality

Advanced phase functionality lets you keep track of changes and compare changes in the process over time, allowing you to see improvement in real time.

AI Insights

Every visual is accompanied by AI insights into your data to help you make smarter decisions.

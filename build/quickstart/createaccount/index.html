Create your ControlShark account

ControlShark requires an account to be created for the person who will administrate the system for your organisation.

Feel free to skip this section if you have already created your account.

Create an account for the organisation administrator#

  1. Navigate to https://app.controlshark.net/accounts/register/
  2. Enter your details and click 'Create my account'

  1. You will receive an email with a link to verify your account

  1. After clicking the link you will be notified that your email address has been verified successfully and you will be able to login

Payment options#

You can get started with ControlShark straight away by simply using our online payments system. This will give you instant access and setup your organisation dashboard using the company name you entered during the sign up process. Alternatively you can request a quote from the ControlShark team if you prefer to pay by invoice, this usually takes a few hours for us to respond.

When you login for the first time you can choose to either pay online or request a quote.

Online payment#

If you choose to pay online you will be taken to our secure payment portal.

Request a quote#

If you choose request a quote we will automatically recieve your request and get back to you usually within a few hours.

Once payment is complete you will be logged into your organisation dashboard.

Ready to go

This is all you need to do to get started with ControlShark! You can also add more users to your organisation later on.

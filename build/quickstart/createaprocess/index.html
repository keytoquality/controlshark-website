Processes

What is a Process?#

A Process is any function, requirement or output of an organisation that can be measured.

Some examples could be:

  • Tolerances for quality control purposes
  • Environmental factors like temperature, humidity or noise
  • Customer service requirements like turnaround or delivery times
  • Percentage yield of passed versus failed items
  • Costs

Create a Process#

Navigate to the organisation dashboard. Locate the appropriate Process Group and click on "add new process".

Enter the details for the new process and click save.

Organisation Dashboard

Dashboard Overview#

The organisation dashboard is the main screen that is displayed after you log in to your ControlShark account. The dashboard shows all the processes for your organisation, which can be grouped into seperate areas using process groups.

What is a Process?#

A Process is any function, requirement or output of an organisation that can be measured.

Some examples could be:

  • Tolerances for quality control purposes
  • Environmental factors like temperature, humidity or noise
  • Customer service requirements like turnaround or delivery times
  • Percentage yield of passed versus failed items
  • Costs

What is a Process Group?#

A Process Group is a collection of related processes. It's a way to organise your processes on the organisation dashboard. You can choose to name your process groups in whichever way you want.

For example, processes could be seperated by:

  • Sites
  • Business Functions / Departments
  • Products
  • Projects
  • Services
  • Teams
  • Customers
  • Suppliers

Or any situation that is relevent to your organisation and industry.

While similar software products are only oriented towards product manufacturing, ControlShark has a simple and configurable structure that enables it to be applied to almost any business in any industry.

Process Groups

What is a Process Group?#

A Process Group is a collection of related processes. It's a way to organise your processes on the organisation dashboard. You can choose to name your process groups in whichever way you want.

For example, processes could be seperated by:

  • Sites
  • Business Functions / Departments
  • Products
  • Projects
  • Services
  • Teams
  • Customers
  • Suppliers

Or any situation that is relevent to your organisation and industry.

While similar software products are only oriented towards product manufacturing, ControlShark has a simple and configurable structure that enables it to be applied to almost any business in any industry.

Create a new Process Group#

Navigate to the organisation dashboard and click on the "add new process group" button, which always appears at the end of your existing process groups.

On the next page that appears, type a name and description for the new process group.

any field marked with an asterix (*) denotes a required field

Once finished, click the save button and you will be returned to the organisation dashboard to see your newly created process group.

Edit an existing Process Group#

Navigate to the organisation dashboard and click on the pencil icon in the top right corner of the process group that you want to edit.

The process group can now be edited or deleted.

Note: to delete a process group, the processes contained within the group will need to be deleted first
